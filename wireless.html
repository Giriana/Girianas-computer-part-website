Wireless Networks

Description

Wireless networks use radio waves to connect devices without cables. They are common in homes and public spaces.

Hardware Needed

  • Wireless router
  • Wireless access points
  • Devices with WiFi capability

Pros and Cons

Pros:

  • No cables needed
  • Easy to add new devices
  • Allows mobility

Cons:

  • Slower than wired sometimes
  • Can have interference
  • Less secure if not properly configured
