Wireless Networks

Connections Without Cables

About Wireless Networks

Wireless networks use radio waves instead of cables. They let you move around.

What You Need

  • Wireless router
  • Wi-Fi in your devices
  • Sometimes extenders

Good and Bad Points

Good:

  • No cables needed
  • Easy to move around
  • Simple setup

Bad:

  • Can be slower
  • Sometimes loses signal
  • Security concerns
