Wired Networks

Description

Wired networks use physical cables to connect devices. They are reliable and fast, commonly used in offices and schools.

Hardware Needed

  • Ethernet cables (Cat5, Cat6)
  • Switches and routers
  • Network Interface Cards
  • Patch panels

Pros and Cons

Pros:

  • Faster speeds
  • More reliable connection
  • Better security

Cons:

  • Less flexible (can't move around)
  • Requires cables everywhere
  • Installation can be difficult
