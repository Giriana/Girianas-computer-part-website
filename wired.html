About Wired Networks

Wired networks use cables to connect devices. They are reliable and fast.

What You Need

  • Ethernet cables
  • Network switches
  • Routers
  • Network cards

Good and Bad Points

Good:

  • Fast speeds
  • Reliable connection
  • Secure

Bad:

  • Need cables
  • Hard to move devices
  • Can look messy
